About Us

Prime Legal Solutions is a remote law firm based in India, dedicated to delivering cost-effective, high-quality legal support to legal professionals, businesses and small to mid-sized law firms across the UAE, United States and Europe.

We specialize in a wide range of services including litigation support, legal and case law research, contract drafting (including transactional documents), and assistance with Trademarks searches and Disputes and other key legal areas. Our firm ensures precise, efficient, and confidential solutions tailored to your legal needs.

We provide our services at a highly competitive pricing of just $7/hour.
